Chilaquiles

Description

Typical Mexican recipe, based on fried tortilla pieces with salsa and toppings.
This recipe is tasty, cheap, simple to prepare and can be customised as desired, making it a great choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

For the Sauce

  • Ripe roma tomatoes
  • White or yellow onion
  • Guajillo chiles
  • 3 chiles de Ã¡rbol
  • Garlic cloves
  • Chicken or vegetable bullion
  • Seasonings: Kosher salt, dried oregano, black pepper

For the Tortillas

  • Corn tortillas
  • Canola & avocado oil

Toppings

Your choice.

Examples:

  • Sour cream
  • Fried eggs
  • Avocado
  • Sliced onions (red better)
  • Cilantro
  • Radishes
  • Pulled chicken

Steps

For the Sauce

  1. In a small saucepan, add two cups of water, chiles, tomatoes, onion & garlic. Over medium-high heat, bring the water to a soft boil,
    then lower heat to medium-low. Cook for 7 minutes or until tomatoes are soft.
  2. In a blender, add one cup of the cooking water, the chiles, tomatoes, onion, garlic, bullion, plus all the spices. Puree for one or
    two minutes ultil everything is pureed well.
  3. Add one tablespoon oil to a saucepan/skillet and heat over medium heat. Add the tomato/chile puree. Turn the heat to low, and simmer,
    stirring often, for about 5 minutes, until the sauce darkens and thickens a little bit.
  4. Taste and adjust salt & pepper.

For the Tortillas

  1. While the sauce is cooking, fry or bake the tortillas.
  2. In the saucepan where the sauce is cooking, strir in the tortilla chips, and fold until the tortilla is coated with the sauce.
    Remove from heat.
  3. Serve immediately to prevent tortillas from getting soggy!

Additional

  • Add the toppings of your choice.
  • Serve with refried beans.
