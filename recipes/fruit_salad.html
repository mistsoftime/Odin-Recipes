Fruit Salad

Description

Hearty bowl of fruit salad, as simple as it sounds!
Nothing for a good summer afternoon like a large bowl of fruit salad after a barbecue with friends, hope you enjoy this recipe!

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • 2/3 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Salad:

  • 2 cups cubed fresh pineapple
  • 2 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 3 kiwi fruit, peeled and sliced
  • 3 bananas sliced
  • 2 oranges, peeled and sectioned
  • 1 cup seedless grapes
  • 2 cups blueberries

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. For the sauce: Brinc orange juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, orange zest, and lemon zest to a boil in a saucepan over
    medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract. Set aside to cool.
  4. For the salad: Layer fruit in a large, clear glass bowl in this order: pineapple, strawberries, kiwi fruit, bananas, oranges,
    grapes, and blueberries. Pour cooled sauce over fruit: cover and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours before serving.
