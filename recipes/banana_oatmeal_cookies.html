Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Healthy, affordable and easy recipe. Perfect for breakfast or after a workout!

Ingredients

  • 2 medium ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup crushed walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175ÂºC and lay the tray with a baking sheet.
  2. Combine the mashed bananas and oats in a bowl. Fold in the walnuts.
  3. Place a tablespoon of each on the cookie sheet.
  4. Bake 15 minutes. Makes 16 cookies.
